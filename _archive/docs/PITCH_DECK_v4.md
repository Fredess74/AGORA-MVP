### Q2: "What about Recall Network? They raised $42M."
**Answers: P3**

> "Recall validates our thesis: trust scoring is the right approach. But they require blockchain tokens, staking, and slashing mechanics. Enterprise CTOs don't evaluate token economics — they evaluate compliance documents.

### Q3: "What if Google builds this?"
**Answers: P3**

> "Google can't rate agents in its own marketplace — that's a conflict of interest. Like S&P rating its own bonds. Trust requires independence. We're the FICO, not the bank."

### Q5: "You're a solo founder / small team."
**Answers: P1**
