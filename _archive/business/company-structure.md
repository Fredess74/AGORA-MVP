## 5. DECISION FRAMEWORK

### For Solo Founder Decisions

#### Speed over perfection
- **Rule**: If decision is reversible, make it in 24 hours
- **Example**: Which tech stack? → Pick one, move on

#### 80/20 Focus
- **Rule**: What 20% gives 80% of value? Do that.
- **Example**: Perfect docs vs. working product? → Working product first

#### Customer Signal
- **Rule**: When unsure, ask customers (or potential ones)
- **Example**: Which feature next? → Ask beta users

#### Energy Management
- **Rule**: Hardest work during peak energy time
- **Example**: Coding in morning when fresh

## 11. FOUNDER WELLBEING

### Prevent Burnout

| Practice | Frequency | Why |
|----------|-----------|-----|
| **Day off** | 1/week minimum | Recovery |
| **Exercise** | 3-4x/week | Energy & clarity |
| **Social time** | Weekly | Not all work |
| **Sleep** | 7-8 hours | Performance |
| **Vacation** | 1 week/quarter | Perspective |

### Warning Signs
- [ ] Working 80+ hours every week
- [ ] Can't stop thinking about work
- [ ] Health declining
- [ ] Relationships suffering
- [ ] No progress despite effort

### When to Stop
It's okay to stop if:
- Market not responding after 18 months
- Running out of money with no path
- Health seriously affected
- Lost passion entirely

**But**: Give it at least 12-18 months of real effort first.
