## 4. Transaction Volumes & Protocol Adoption

| Protocol | Function | Key Metric | Status |
|----------|----------|-----------|--------|
| **MCP** | Connectivity | 97M+ monthly SDK downloads; 17,000+ servers | De facto standard |
| **x402** | M2M Settlement | 50M+ cumulative txs; ~$28K real daily vol; $0.20 avg | 40-50% wash trading |
| **Circle Nano** | Micropayments | 140M txs; $43M volume; $0.31 avg; 400K+ active agents | Strongest real data |
| **Google UCP/AP2** | Commerce Routing | 60+ enterprise partners (Shopify, Walmart, Visa, etc.) | Open standard |
| **Mastercard Agent Pay** | Intent Verification | Integrated into Copilot Checkout | Cryptographic audit trail |

### Circle Nanopayments — Strongest Evidence of Real Activity
- **140 million payments** by AI agents over 9 months
- **$43 million** total volume
- **98.6%** settled in USDC
- **$0.31** average transaction
- **400,000+ AI agents** with active purchasing power

### x402 Reality Check
- Headline: 50M+ transactions, $600M annualized
- Reality: **~$28K real daily volume**, $0.20 avg transaction
- **40-50% is wash trading** (bots trading with themselves for airdrops)
- Technical payment rail WORKS, but trust/discovery layer is severely lagging

## 8. The Missing Layer (Report Conclusion)

> "The market has largely established HOW agents connect (MCP) and HOW they pay
> (x402, AP2, USDC). The ecosystem's success now hinges entirely on establishing
> WHO TO TRUST."

Key evidence: With 40-50% wash trading on x402 and 400K+ autonomous agents already transacting, the lack of behavioral trust scoring is the **single largest bottleneck** to realizing the $5T agentic commerce projection.
